Forsen's DMCA free playlist

This is a simple media player which comes with a nice playlist full of hand-picked songs, including game OSTs, memes, etc.

The original purpose of this playlist was to provide streamers with DMCA-free music. Especially those who can't be bothered to take several minutes to setup their OBS audio properly and play whatever music they want.

You can use your own playlist. Click the "Change playlist" button and provide a YouTube link to shuffle your favorite songs.
Features
  • As simple as it gets - no extra buttons
  • Unavailable songs are automatically skipped
  • Shuffler uses all songs (not just first 200)
  • Restores your last shuffle order and last played song - don't repeat yourself!
  • New songs are automatically appended to the list as the playlist gets updated.
  • Custom shuffle mechanism: is really random, I promise!
  • You can change the default playlist - shuffle your favorite songs!
Controls
  • Volume control: slide to adjust sound level
  • Play/pause: use to quickly start or stop current song
  • Next song: click to skip current song
  • Reshuffle: click to reshuffle songs. You can also order an auto-reshuffle on each visit, if you want.
  • Change playlist: you can enter a link to YouTube playlist, and the shuffler will play your songs.
